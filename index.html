About


My name is Michael Zen-hang Lui, and I want to educate children and parents on the importance of self-defense.

I envision a world where everyone has access to knowledge and opportunities that help them become people can feel safe and capable under any circumstances.

Whether I'm teaching a 3 year old how to motivate themselves to focus, or chatting with a teenager about the latest episode of anime, or sparring for my life with my peers and masters, I am dedicating my time and effort into become a better martial artist, and to share everything I know with anyone who will listen to me.
