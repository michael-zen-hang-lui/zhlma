Frequently Asked Questions

Is ZHLMA safe for my children and myself?

I aim to provide a safe and informed learning environment, however, like all physical activities, you must be willing to accept the risk of injury. While I never force anyone to do anything they don't want to do, that doesn't mean I won't push you past your comfort zone.

Are ther plus 1, travel, and extra fees?

All locations that more than 30 minutes by public transport or a 15 minute walk from SHEUNG WAN MTR station will incur a travel free of 100HKD. If you'd like to have a plus 1 to your private class, the fee is 100HKD.

Does training involve intimate physical contact?

Certain aspects of training may include indirect by intimate physical contact. All scenario based exercises will be done with informed consent, and I will use the absolute minimal amount of touch possible, but accidental collisions may occur.

How effective is the training?

What I try to do is provide students with the toolkit to become effective learners of martial arts and self defense. I will make sure that you get a basic grasp of the absolute fundamentals that are proven to have a high percentage rate of success for beginners.

Do I need prior experience to train?

ZHLMA is completely beginner friendly. If I can teach a 6 year hold how to sit still and focus, I can teach a grown up how to throw their first punch and escape their first wrist grab.

Is there an age limit for the kids?

I prefer to use the ability of a student to observe and retain information in a given context as the key metric. While I make the classes fun and interactive for the kids, it's not a just a play group, so easily distracted kids may need a private class. Similarly, some kids thrive in a group and will be bored to tears in a private class, so I encourage all parents to give both a go.
