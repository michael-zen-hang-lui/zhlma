About

About


My name is Michael Zen-hang Lui, and I want to educate children and parents on the importance of self-defense.


I envision a world where everyone has access to knowledge and opportunities that help them become people who can feel safe, calm and capable under any circumstances.


Whether I'm teaching a 3 year old how to motivate themselves to focus, or chatting with a teenager about the latest episode of anime, or sparring for my life with my peers and masters, I am dedicating my time and effort into become a better martial artist, and to share everything I know with anyone who will listen to me.


While I'm currently based in Hong Kong running a one-man show, I aspire to reach further, far further than I ever could on my own, and I want you to help me.


Whether it's by being a direct customer, or by sharing this website or my posts on social media, or just by being curious and shooting me an email or message, I encourage you to take the first step in becoming a martial artist if you haven't already.


What I Do

What I Do


I offer private, semi-private, and small group classes, bespoke to the students needs.


Want me to tire your kid out on a weekday night? You got it. Need to learn how to defend yourself against aggressive posturing without resorting to violence? I can do that. What to just blow off some steam in an interesting way? I'll come up with a different class and plan for you that'll get it all out of you.


Whether you're a veteran combat-sport competitor or practicioner, or the aforementioned 3 year-old, I will pay attention to your needs and interests, and do my best to serve your goals as a martial-artists.


I'm no black belt. I'm not a master. I'm not a sifu/sensei/tuhon or any other title. I'm just someone who wants to make the world a better place the best way I can, by learning from all the black belts, masters, sifus, senseis, tuhons, who want to share their art with the world, and share it with you so that you can be exposed to as many tools as possible, and decide what you want for yourself.
