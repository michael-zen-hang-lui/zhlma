About

About


My name is Michael Zen-hang Lui, and I want to educate children and parents on the importance of self-defense.


I envision a world where everyone has access to knowledge and opportunities that help them become people who can feel safe, calm and capable under any circumstances.


Whether I'm teaching a 5 year-old tricks to stay focused, or chatting with a teenager about the latest episode of an anime, or sparring for my life with my masters, I am dedicating my life to become a better martial artist and to share everything I know.


While I'm currently based in Hong Kong running a one-man show, I aspire to reach further, far further than I ever could on my own, and I want you to help me, so I encourage you to take the first step in becoming a martial artist if you haven't already.


What I Do

What I Do


I offer private, semi-private, and small group classes, bespoke to the students needs.


Does your kid need exercise? Do you want to learn how to defend yourself at your own pace and style? Need to just blow off some steam in an interesting way? I can cater to all of those needs and more with a martial arts twist. Whether you're a veteran combat-sport competitor or practicioner, or the aforementioned 5 year-old, I will pay attention to your needs and interests, and do my best to serve your goals as a martial-artists.


I'm no black belt. I'm not a master. I'm not a sifu/sensei/tuhon or any other title. I'm just someone who wants to make the world a better place the best way I can, by learning from all the black belts, masters, sifus, senseis, tuhons, who want to share their art with the world, and share it with you so that you can be exposed to as many tools as possible, and decide what you want for yourself.
